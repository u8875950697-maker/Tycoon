# Monetization Overview

## Philosophy
- Keep sessions short, satisfying, and free from hard paywalls.
- Spending remains optional and focused on cosmetics or quality-of-life boosts.

## Currency Roles
- **Coins:** Primary loop rewards for upgrades and slot unlocks.
- **Mana:** Fuels magical interactions and temporary boosts.
- **Essence:** Rare crafting material tied to hybrid trees and elixirs.
- **Gems:** Optional premium currency with limited, player-friendly usage.

## Gem Sources
- Rare fruit drops.
- World clear bonuses.
- Daily login reward (small drip).
- Rewarded ads (opt-in only).
- Optional purchase path reserved for cosmetics or time-savers.

## Rewarded Ad Touchpoints
1. **Double Harvest:** After a run, doubles coins from the final 60 seconds.
2. **Slot Token:** Grants one additional slot unlock token, limited to once per day.
3. **Speed Boost:** Provides a 5-minute haste buff, once per world run.

### Cooldowns & Limits
- Global 10-minute cooldown between rewarded ads.
- Daily cap of 3 ad views.
- Each prompt includes a clear "No thanks" option.

## Ethics Guardrails
- No forced interruptions during active play.
- No energy timers or hard progression gates.
- Clear communication on rewards and optional nature of purchases.

## Configuration
- Toggle rewarded ad availability via `Config/Monetization/flags.md` (placeholders only).
