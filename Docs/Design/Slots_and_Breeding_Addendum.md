# Slots & Breeding Addendum

## Tree Slots
- Players start each world with **3 active slots**.
- Additional slots unlock via Coins in early tiers and optional Gems in later tiers, up to **12 slots per world**.
- Rewarded ads grant **one slot token per day** as an alternative path for non-spenders.

## Breeding Costs & Limits
- Breeding consumes **Coins plus a small Essence fee** per attempt.
- Players receive **3 free breeding attempts per session**; further attempts require waiting for a cooldown or paying a **small Gem fee**.
- Offline time does **not** progress breeding cooldowns beyond the standard AFK accrual rules.

## Offline Interaction
- Breeding outcomes do **not** roll while offline to prevent passive reroll abuse.
- Trees that are planted continue to fruit according to the offline formula outlined in the AFK rewards spec.
