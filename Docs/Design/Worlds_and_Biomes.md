## Starter World Set
1. **Meadow**
   - Native trees: Wood Tree, Water Birch.
   - Hazard: Mild pests that slow growth.
   - Temporary buff: Morning Dew (+growth).
   - Visual motif: Bright greens, rolling hills.
2. **Swamp**
   - Native trees: Water Birch, Algae Poplar.
   - Hazard: Higher disease chance.
   - Temporary buff: Bog Bloom (reduced water use).
   - Visual motif: Misty blues and murky pools.
3. **Volcano**
   - Native trees: Lava Ash, Ember Palm.
   - Hazard: High water consumption.
   - Temporary buff: Magma Surge (rare yield spike).
   - Visual motif: Charcoal ground with glowing cracks.
4. **Ice Coast**
   - Native trees: Frost Pine, Glimmer Spruce.
   - Hazard: Slower growth rate.
   - Temporary buff: Aurora Veil (rare drop multiplier).
   - Visual motif: Cool blues, ice floes.
5. **Dream Isles**
   - Native trees: Dream Willow, Prism Palm.
   - Hazard: Mana storms that scramble timers.
   - Temporary buff: Lucid Focus (crit window extension).
   - Visual motif: Pastel skies and floating isles.

## Exit Conditions & Rewards
- Typical exit goals: fill N tree slots, harvest X rare fruit, or craft one elixir.
- Clearing a world awards a world badge plus a small permanent meta bonus tailored to its theme.
