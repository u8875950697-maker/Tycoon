# Rewards and Feedback Loops

## Positive Feedback Stack
- Fruit pops with satisfying audio cues and minimal screen shake.
- Sparkle bursts highlight critical harvests.
- Coin trails arc toward the currency bar for clarity.
- World completion shows a summary card with three key stats and a "Luckiest Drop" callout.

## Streak Bonuses
- Completing three runs in a row unlocks the **Starlit Boost**, granting a free 5-minute buff on the next session.
- Streak progress displays subtly on the world select screen to encourage short repeat runs without pressure.
